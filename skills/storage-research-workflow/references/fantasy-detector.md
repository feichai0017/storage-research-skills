# Fantasy Detector

This is the strict anti-hype stage.
Use it to reject "玄幻课题" before implementation starts.

## Core Question

Is this a real systems contribution, or are we projecting a paper onto weak evidence?

## Fantasy Signals

### Fake Problem

- the problem is mostly fashionable language
- no one concrete pays the cost
- the issue disappears once the scope is drawn honestly

### Fake Novelty

- new names for known mechanisms
- "first" claim based on wording instead of boundary
- the only difference is combining two familiar ideas

### Fake Feasibility

- requires traces, infrastructure, or scale we do not have
- relies on an unrealistic baseline
- would take months before the first falsifiable result

### Fake Evaluation

- benchmark only shows the happy path
- semantics differ across systems but numbers are compared directly
- strong claims are supported only by microbenchmarks

### Fake Venue Ambition

- workshop evidence with top-tier framing
- narrow engineering tweak framed as architectural innovation
- artifact quality too small for the claimed contribution

## Pass Conditions

An idea passes the fantasy detector only if:
- the problem survives simplification
- the boundary is still distinct after comparing with closest prior work
- the first real artifact is buildable
- the evaluation story is believable
- the likely venue band matches the strongest supported claim
